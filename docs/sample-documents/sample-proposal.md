## Scope of Work

Contoso Construction Ltd requests professional construction management and advisory services for Phase 2 of the Riverfront Office Tower renovation project. The scope includes:

1. **Structural Assessment Review** — Independent review of the existing structural assessment report for floors 8–15
2. **Permit Coordination** — Assistance with City of Portland building permit applications and compliance reviews
3. **Contractor Oversight** — Ongoing oversight of subcontractor work quality and schedule adherence
4. **Safety Compliance** — Weekly safety audits aligned with OSHA 1926 construction standards
5. **Progress Reporting** — Bi-weekly progress reports to the Contoso executive team

### Out of Scope

- Architectural design services
- Direct construction labor
- Environmental remediation (handled by separate contractor)
